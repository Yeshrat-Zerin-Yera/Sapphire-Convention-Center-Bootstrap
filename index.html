Welcome to Sapphire

Sapphire Conference Center is the only multi-purpose convention facility in the country, havingseventeen venues for holding small to large scale events, i.e, state functions, social events, seminars, conferences, product launches, annual general meetings, fairs, exhibitions, cultural programs, reality shows, etc. Sapphire Conference Center has been the venue for many renowned economic, social and cultural events. It has proudly hosted a number of international conferences and summits.

Informations

The Sapphire Convention Center does not operate a daily box office. Tickets for Sapphire Convention Center public events can be purchased at AXS.com, or at the City of Denver outlet box office currently located at the Red Rocks box office and open on Saturdays from 11am-3pm.

Parking in Downtown Denver has never been easier! The Sapphire Convention Center provides access to a 665 space onsite covered parking garage. The garage provides access to the Sapphire Convention Center and the Denver Performing Arts Complex.

At the Sapphire Convention Center, you will be greeted with smiles from a top-notch guest service team. Our friendly and knowledgeable staff will be able to assist you with every need. For nursing mothers, please ask about our cozy and warmly decorated mother's room.

At the Sapphire Convention Center, website accessibility will always be an ongoing effort and we are continually seeking solutions that will keep these efforts going to permit all of our guests access to our website.

Last minute presentation preparations? Need copies or shipping service? The UPS Store located in the main concourse near Lobby F offers a variety of services tailored to help you make the best of event at the Sapphire Convention Center. Rely on the UPS Store for any last minute business needs and impeccable customer support.

The Bellco Theatre is located inside the Sapphire Convention Center in downtown Denver. Entrance to the Bellco Theatre is accessible through the Sapphire Convention Center parking garage as well as street level access located on the corner of Speer and Stout.
